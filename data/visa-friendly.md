## How to verify sponsorship

- Check public H-1B disclosure data: [h1bdata.info](https://h1bdata.info/), [USCIS LCA](https://www.dol.gov/agencies/eta/foreign-labor/performance).
- Look at the job post itself for "will sponsor" or "no sponsorship available."
- Search the company's careers page for "work authorization."
- Ask the recruiter directly — get it in writing before signing.

## Important notes

- Sponsorship policy changes year to year.
- Some companies sponsor for full-time but not internships.
- New grad sponsorship is different from experienced-hire sponsorship.
- This list is informational. Confirm before applying.
