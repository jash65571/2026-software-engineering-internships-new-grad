## How to verify sponsorship

- Check public H-1B disclosure data (e.g. USCIS LCA filings, h1bdata.info).
- Look at the job post itself for "will sponsor" or "no sponsorship available."
- Search the company's careers page for "work authorization."
- Ask in the interview if the role description is unclear. Do not assume.

## Important notes

- Sponsorship policy can change year to year.
- Some companies sponsor for full-time but not internships.
- New grad sponsorship is different from experienced-hire sponsorship.
- This list is informational only. Confirm before applying.
