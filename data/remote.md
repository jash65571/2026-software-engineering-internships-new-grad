## Tips for remote job applications

- Confirm time zone requirements before applying.
- "Remote" sometimes means "remote within a single state" — read the fine print.
- Some "remote" roles require US work authorization without sponsorship.
- Look at the company's engineering team to see if remote work is real.
- Async-first companies (GitLab, Automattic, Supabase) expect strong written communication.
