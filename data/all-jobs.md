## How to use this page

1. Scan the table for roles that match your skills, work authorization, and location.
2. Open the apply link and search for the role on the company's site.
3. Run the JD through the [Job Fit Checker](https://www.tryapplynow.com/tools/job-fit-checker) before tailoring.
4. Find a referral with the [Email Finder](https://www.tryapplynow.com/tools/email-finder).
5. Tailor your resume with the [Resume Keyword Checker](https://www.tryapplynow.com/tools/resume-keyword-checker).
6. Apply and track.
