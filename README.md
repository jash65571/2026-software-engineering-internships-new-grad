A regularly updated list of 2026 software engineering internships and new grad roles for students, recent grads, and early-career developers.

This repo focuses on real job-search help, not mass applying. Use it to find roles, check fit, improve your resume, and apply with more intent.

## How to use this repo

1. Open the internship or new grad job list.
2. Pick roles that match your skills, location, and work authorization.
3. Paste the job description into an ATS resume checker.
4. Compare your resume against the role.
5. Tailor your resume before applying.
6. Find one employee or recruiter contact when possible.
7. Track the job after applying.

## Submit a job

Open a pull request or issue with:

- Company name
- Role title
- Location
- Internship or new grad
- Apply link
- Date posted if known
- Any notes about sponsorship, remote work, or deadline

Please do not submit expired jobs, fake jobs, or recruiter spam.
